Welcome to PyPegase's documentation!
====================================


We present a code, that focuses on predicting colors and luminosities of Single
Stellar population according to different assumptions. In particular, we offer a
simplified recipe to rapidly generate stochasticly sampled populatons with
controled approximation errors.
