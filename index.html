About Me

I'm a Postdoctoral fellow in the Astronomy department at University of Washington (Seattle). My current research is mostly oriented towards understanding star clusters in galaxies, and primarily focusing on low-mass clusters to eventually constrain the dissolution theory of star clusters. However, I also contribute to many data modeling, especially using probabilistic inferences in astronomy but also other domains of physics. My current paying bill project is the Panchromatic Hubble Andromeda Treasury (PHAT) survey, an HST multi-cycle mapping 1/3 or M31 from UV to NIR at HST best resolution.
